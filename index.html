LION

African lions are the most social of all big cats and live together in groups or “prides.” A pride consists of about 15 lions. ... Male lions defend the pride's territory while females do most of the hunting. Despite this, the males eat first.

  • Scientific Name : Panthera Leo
  • Average Length : 5.6 to 8.2 ft
  • Average Lifespan : 10-14 years
  • Habitat: Desert, grasslands in Africa and Asia

The lion is a species in the family Felidae and a member of the genus Panthera. It is considered to be the second-largest living cat species after the tiger, and is easily recognisable by the distinctive mane of the male.
